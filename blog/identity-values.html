..Explain a situation where you have had an ethical decision to make. Discuss how you weighed up the values involved in that decision, the decision you made and the consequences of the action you took..

I was in the exercise yard one day and I witnessed somebody get knocked out cold. He fell flat on his face. Everybody in the yard saw what happend yet did nothing and carried on like nothing had happened. I noticed the person was struggling to breathe. He was lying there unconscious face first into the concrete. I thought to myself maybe I should carry on and leave him there. Then this chapter in this book I had read from Dr Paul Wood came into my head. The chapter was about “how violence had become normal to him”. In that moment, I knew this was a real chance for me to do something new or different. It hit me that violence had become normal to me too. This is not because im a violent person rather I’d been In a violent environment for such a long while. So I went over and helped this guy by trying to put him into the recovery position. Some people started yelling at me to leave him and just let him die. I almost listened to them yet I felt this strong urge come over me that was to do the right thing. So I did it by rolling him over. when that happened he started to breathe and I felt a sense of relief come over me. Now I knew I had done something right for the first time in a long while. If I had’nt read that book I probably would’ve just left him there. This was a significant ethical decision that has changed the way I act. I value helping others remain safe and well.

..Describe how your culture has influenced your values and identity..

My Maori culture is what keeps me grounded and reminds me of how unique I am. My life experiences being Maori have taught me to be family orientated, respectful, humble, kind and caring. but also to work hard and always be aware of myself, others and my surroundings In our culture being respectful means looking after the environment as well as looking up to our elders. Our elders have passed down their knowledge and language to make us a strong culture. Humility means we are modest and dignified in the way in which we behave. I strive to be kind and caring in all my interactions with others.

Using the evidence gathered, describe your strengths and limitations.

My strengths are my determination to achieve at a high level. Im always learning to improve myself. My Caring nature allows me to be vulnerable which makes it easy for me to connect and relate with others who i meet. I have a Positive attitude and open mind that helps me push through any obstacles I come across. My Limitations would be that I lack the confidence to put myself out there to a big audience.
