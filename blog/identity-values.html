Values, Identity & Strengths.

..Explain a situation where you have had an ethical decision to make. the decision you made and the consequences of the action you took..


I was in the exercise yard one day and I witnessed somebody get knocked out cold. He fell flat on his face. Everybody saw what happend yet did nothing and carried on like nothing had happened. I noticed the person was struggling to breathe. He was lying there unconscious face first into the concrete. I thought to myself maybe I should carry on and leave him there. Then this chapter in this book I had read from Dr Paul Wood came into my head. The chapter was about “how violence had become normal to him”. In that moment, I knew this was a real chance for me to do something new or different. It hit me that violence had become normal to me too. This is not because im a violent person rather I’d been In a violent environment for such a long while. So I went over and helped this guy by trying to put him into the recovery position. Some people started yelling at me to leave him and just let him die. I almost listened to them yet I felt this strong urge come over me that was to do the right thing. So I did it by rolling him over. when that happened he started to breathe and I felt a sense of relief come over me. Now I knew I had done something right for the first time in a long while. If I had’nt read that book I probably would’ve just left him there. This was a significant ethical decision that has changed the way I act. I value helping others remain safe and well.

..Describe how your culture has influenced your values and identity..


My Maori culture is what keeps me grounded and reminds me of how unique I am. My life experiences being Maori have taught me to be family orientated, respectful, humble, kind and caring. but also to work hard and always be aware of myself, others and my surroundings. In our culture being respectful means looking after the environment as well as looking up to our elders. Our elders have passed down their knowledge and language to make us a strong culture. Humility means we are modest and dignified in the way in which we behave. I strive to be kind and caring in all my interactions with others.

..Using the evidence gathered, describe your strengths and limitations..


My strengths are my determination to achieve at a high level. Im always learning to improve myself. My Caring nature allows me to be vulnerable which makes it easy for me to connect and relate with others who i meet. I have a Positive attitude and open mind that helps me push through any obstacles I come across. My Limitations would be that I lack the confidence to put myself out there to a big audience. I’m a quiet and reserved type of person. Therefore I often struggle to ask for help and like to sort out problems on my own. To work on these limitations I realise I need to challenge myself to try new things and to ask for assistance.

..Share an example from your experience of where you were trying to work productively with others, but there was resistance or tension..


I was given a fitness coaching role for a rugby league team. So I created a fitness, strength and conditioning plan. Anyone of any size or fitness level could do. Fitness is important when you’re out on the field. I told the team they had to meet a “fitness requirement level” before they could play. Most of the team were happy, but others were not. They thought being big and strong is enough. So when the team had their first game, they got smashed because half the team werent fit enough.


..Discuss the strategies you used at that time, how effective they were, and your reflections on what other strategies you would try now, and why..


after that first game a stratergy i used was. before training started we would open up with a karakia (prayer) to lighten the mood. Team bonding exercises and Team encouragement. getting the whole team to encourage one another. starting strong together and finishing strong together. Reminding players there were only limited spots and they had to earn thier place. Having those stratergies in place really helped the team to bond / gel and come together. training become enjoyable and fun.
