Problem solving

.. What was the problem..


I got stuck on the fizzbuzz challenge. Fizzbuzz is a challenge that requires certain numbers to return a certain “string” (word). To figure out what to do i googled what it was about and found some ideas about how to approach this challenge. Checked out the foundations channel and found some ideas there too. Then i realised id been thinking to hard into this challenge and the solution was, well not simple but do-able. Through out the challenge i found my anxiety levels start to come down. There was also a solution file which really helped me understand what i was looking at. Having a solution file there made the fizzbuzz challenge alot better for me. What i learnt from this problem was: not to overthink things.


i tried all of them but the ones i felt worked well for me were:


  • Googling things.
  • console logging.
  • just giving it a go.
  • asking the foundations community..

I feel my reflections are starting to improve. I am starting to share more and be more in depth when reflecting on my foundations journey.
