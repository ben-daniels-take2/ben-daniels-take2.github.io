Emotional Intelligence


..What is Emotional Intelligence? ..


Emotional and social skills that influence the way we express ourselves, develop and maintain social relationships, and cope with challenges. identify and regulate one's emotions and understand the emotions the others.

..How is it different to IQ?..


Iq is more about your academic abillities. Eq focuses more on your emotional side. How well you interact and connect with others. I believe without IQ everyone would funtion like robots. Robots have no emotion or emotional connection to anything or anyone.

..Why is emotional intelligence important?..


Research shows that emotional intelligence is a key indicator of human performance and development. People higher in emotional intelligence communicate effectively, form strong relationships, and create powerful coping strategies.
