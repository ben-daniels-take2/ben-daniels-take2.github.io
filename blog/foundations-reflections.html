Reflections

.. What are the role of values, empathy, and self awareness in learning and programming?..


They play a key part to you being able to learn, listen, connect and communicate effectively. theres no way you can form and build long lasting meaningful relationships without learning about any of these roles.


What has surprised you the most about the core learning?


Being able to reflect on a deeper level and express myself as if im talking to a real person in front of me. The amount of work and effort Dev Academy have put into core. All the learnings i took away from core material. Seeing how everything i learned you can apply in everyday life.


What were the most challenging aspects of the core learning?


Having to find someone to pair up with for the listening and looping execises. Im shy and lack the confidence to put myself out there. But i did in the end to challenge myself. Other than that core learning was good for me and i didnt find anything to challenging.


Why do you think that we, a programming school, are spending so much time focusing on core learning in a web development bootcamp course?.


Learning core skills are important to have and maintain. Especially when working with others. Being able to communicate and connect with others makes it alot easier to collaborate and problem solve when coding and programming. You could be the best programmer in the world but stil be the shittiest person ever if you dont know how to connect with others. You cant programme human skills but you can learn them.


Does the time you spent studying core learning here feel like a waste of time? Should you have just used that time to practise programming instead?


no. having both tech and core was a good balance for me. I enjoyed being able to reflect after each sprint. At first my reflecting was surface level but as the weeks went on i started to reflect more in depth. I liked being able to sharpen back up on human skills through core learning
